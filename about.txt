About the project
=================

== About

image:logo/logo.png["The LibreMesh logo",role="right"]

LibreMesh is a community and an open project, so anyone can join us. We consider anyone using the LibreMesh firmware as part of this community.
The development is mainly carried on by a group of volunteers, which we identify as a team.
